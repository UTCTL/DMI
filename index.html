The DMI bootcamp is a multimedia design and development training program for UT students. Students will have the opportunity to learn from among a core set of digital media technologies including graphic design, animation, video production, motion graphics, 3D modeling, web development, and mobile development. Students will also collaborate with other students, learn multimedia production best practices.
